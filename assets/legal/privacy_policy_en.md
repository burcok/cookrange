# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how **Cookrange** ("Cookrange", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses,
shares, and protects your personal data when you use the Cookrange mobile application and related
services (the "App"). We are committed to processing your data lawfully, fairly, and transparently in
accordance with the **Turkish Personal Data Protection Law No. 6698 ("KVKK")** and the **EU General
Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)**, applying the stricter standard where they differ.

Cookrange handles **health and fitness data** (such as body metrics, allergies, and dietary
information). This is treated as a special category of personal data and is processed only with your
explicit consent and with heightened safeguards.

## 1. Who We Are (Data Controller)

The data controller responsible for your personal data is Cookrange, operated by Burak Dereli. For any
privacy question or to exercise your rights, contact us at **privacy@cookrangeapp.com**.

## 2. The Data We Collect

We collect only what we need to provide the App. Categories:

- **Account & identity:** email address, display name, profile photo, and authentication identifier
  (created via email, Google, or Apple sign-in).
- **Health & fitness data (sensitive):** height, weight, gender, date of birth, activity level,
  allergies, dietary restrictions, disliked foods, goals, food logs, exercise logs, and weight/
  hydration tracking.
- **Location data (sensitive):** approximate device location, only when you actively use features such
  as "gyms near me" or GPS gym check-in. Location used for "gyms near me" is processed on your device
  to sort gyms by distance and is **not transmitted to or stored on our servers**.
- **Social & content data:** posts, comments, reactions, chat messages, friends/followers, and
  community activity you choose to create.
- **Device & usage data:** device model, operating system, app version, language, and analytics events
  about how you use the App (used to keep the App stable and improve it).
- **AI interaction data:** the prompts you send to the AI assistant and the profile context attached to
  generate meal plans, recipes, and insights.
- **Transaction data:** subscription tier and purchase confirmations for premium features and credit
  packs (payment itself is processed by Apple/Google, not by us).

We do **not** collect more than necessary, and we prefer transient, on-device processing over storage
wherever possible.

## 3. How and Why We Use Your Data (Legal Bases)

| Purpose | Legal basis |
|---|---|
| Create and operate your account; deliver core App features | Performance of a contract |
| Generate personalised meal plans, recipes, analytics, and AI insights | Your explicit consent (health data) |
| Show nearby gyms / GPS check-in | Your explicit consent (location) |
| Send push notifications you opted into | Your consent |
| Keep the App secure; prevent fraud and abuse; diagnose crashes | Our legitimate interests |
| Process subscriptions and keep financial records | Contract / legal obligation |

You may withdraw any consent at any time (see Section 9). Withdrawal does not affect processing carried
out before withdrawal.

## 4. How We Store and Protect Your Data

- Your data is stored on **Google Firebase** (Firestore, Authentication, Storage). Data is encrypted in
  transit.
- **Sensitive health data** is kept in an owner-only area of your account that only you can access.
- We apply least-privilege access rules, app attestation (App Check), and we never embed third-party
  API secrets in the App.
- We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, your
  personal data is permanently removed from our systems within **30 days** (financial records may be
  retained longer where law requires).

## 5. AI and Automated Processing

Meal plans, recipes, and insights are generated using a third-party large-language-model provider
(**OpenRouter**), reached through our secured server so your identity and our keys stay protected. The
prompt text and relevant profile context are sent to generate a result. AI outputs are **estimates for
informational purposes only and are not medical, dietary, or health advice.** We do not make decisions
producing legal or similarly significant effects about you solely by automated means.

## 6. Cookies, SDKs and Tracking

The App does not use browser cookies. It uses software development kits (SDKs) and similar technologies:

- **Firebase Analytics / Crashlytics / Performance** — usage analytics, crash diagnostics, and
  performance monitoring.
- **Firebase Cloud Messaging** — push notifications (with your consent).
- **OpenStreetMap** map tiles — when you open the gym map, tile requests carry your IP address to the
  tile provider.
- **App Tracking Transparency (iOS)** — we ask for your permission before any tracking that would
  require it; you can decline.

## 7. Who We Share Your Data With

We do **not sell** your personal data. We share data only with the service providers (sub-processors)
needed to run the App:

- **Google Firebase** — hosting, authentication, database, storage, messaging, analytics, diagnostics.
- **OpenRouter** — AI inference (receives your prompt and profile context).
- **Apple App Store / Google Play** — in-app purchase processing.
- **OpenStreetMap** — map tile rendering.
- **Payment provider** (future) — coach/affiliate payouts and identity verification.

Community content you post is visible to other authenticated users by design. Your private nutrition
data is never shown to other users.

## 8. International Data Transfers

Our providers (notably Google and OpenRouter) may process data on servers located outside Türkiye and
the EU/EEA. Where this happens, we rely on a lawful transfer mechanism — your explicit consent and/or
appropriate safeguards such as the European Commission's Standard Contractual Clauses — and we disclose
this transfer to you here.

## 9. Your Rights

Under KVKK (Art. 11) and GDPR (Art. 15–22) you have the right to: access your data; correct inaccurate
data; request erasure; restrict or object to processing; data portability; and withdraw consent.

- **Access & portability:** export all your data from **Settings → export your data** (JSON).
- **Erasure:** delete your account and all associated data from **Settings → Delete Account**.
- **Rectification:** edit your profile and preferences in the App.
- **Withdraw consent:** manage notification, privacy, and permission settings; revoke OS permissions
  at any time in your device settings.
- **Make a request:** email **privacy@cookrangeapp.com**. We respond within the statutory period
  (30 days under KVKK; one month under GDPR).

## 10. Children's Privacy

Cookrange is not directed to children under the age required in your country (at least 13, and 16 in
parts of the EU). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children below that age. If you
believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it.

## 11. Data Breaches

If a personal-data breach occurs that is likely to affect your rights, we will notify the competent
authority and, where required, affected users without undue delay and in line with KVKK and GDPR
timelines.

## 12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy. We will notify you of significant changes in the App or by email, and we will
update the "Last updated" date above. Where a change requires new consent, we will ask for it.

## 13. Contact

Questions or requests about your data: **privacy@cookrangeapp.com**.
