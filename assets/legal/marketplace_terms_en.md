charged-back transactions are reversed.

## 7. Conduct & Clients

You must treat clients professionally, honour what you advertise, and protect any client data you
access in line with our Privacy Policy and applicable law. Misconduct may lead to removal and
withholding of payouts.

## 8. Suspension & Termination

Cookrange may suspend or remove a Provider for breach, risk, fraud, or legal reasons. Accrued, undisputed
payouts remain payable subject to verification.
