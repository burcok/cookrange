# Explicit Consent Statement

Last updated: 29 June 2026

This statement obtains your **explicit consent** to process special-category and certain other personal
data under KVKK and GDPR. Assuming you have read the KVKK Clarification Text and the Privacy Policy, you
are **entirely free** to grant or withhold consent to the processing below. If you decline, you may be
unable to use the related feature, but you can keep using core functions. You can **withdraw your
consent at any time**.

## Processing You Consent To

- **Health & fitness data:** processing your height, weight, gender, date of birth, allergies, dietary
  restrictions, and nutrition/exercise logs to deliver personalised meal plans, recipes, analytics, and
  AI insights;
- **Location data:** processing your location in "gyms near me" and GPS check-in to sort gyms and verify
  your presence (this location is not stored on our servers);
- **AI processing:** sending your prompts and relevant profile context to our AI provider (OpenRouter)
  to generate results;
- **International transfer:** processing your data on the overseas servers of providers such as Google
  and OpenRouter, as needed to deliver the service;
- **Notifications and (if any) marketing:** sending the notifications you have approved.

## Scope and Withdrawal

- The above processing is carried out only for the stated purposes and only to the extent necessary.
- You may withdraw consent at any time via in-app settings (notification/privacy/permission management),
  by revoking OS permissions, or by emailing **privacy@cookrangeapp.com**.
- Withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of processing carried out before withdrawal.
- The date of your consent and the version of the text you approved are recorded for accountability.

By starting to use these features, you are deemed to have given explicit consent for the relevant
processing.
